The Great Barrier Reef

located off the coast of Queenland Austrailia the Great barrier is the largest coral reef system in the world ,it stretches over 2300 kilometers(1400miles)and is home to a diverse array of marine life, including more than 1,500 species of fish,411 types of hard coral,and 30 species of whale and dolphin.

The Great Barrier reef is a site of remarkable variety and beauty on the north-east coast of Australia,it contians the world's largest collection of coral reefs ,with 400 types of coral,1300 species of fish and 4,000 types of mollusk _UNESCO World Heritage Centre
